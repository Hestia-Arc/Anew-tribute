STEVE JOBS

AN AMERICAN BUSINESS MAGNATE & CO-FOUNDER OF APPLE

1955-2011

  • 24 FEB 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California, U.S and adopted by Paul and Clara Jobs
  • 1969 - He meets Steve Wozniak, 5 years older,through a mutual friend. Woz and Steve share a love of electronics,Bob Dylan and pranks
  • MAR 1976 -Woz and Steve show the early Apple 1 board at the Homebrew Computer Club
  • 28 AUG 1976 - Steve and Woz show off the Apple 1 at the Personal Computing Festival in Atlantic City, with help from Dan Kottke
  • 1978 -At apple, work starts on the apple 111 and the lisa, while jef raskin begins the book of macintosh
  • MAY 1980 - Apple launches the apple 111, which will prove a disastrous flop
  • JAN 1983 - Launch of the lisa computer. the lisa team later merges with the mac team under steve jobs leadership
  • 8 APR 1983 - Pepsico ceo john sculley becomes apples ceo after having been wooed by steve jobs for several months
  • 17 SEPT 1985 - Steve jobs resigns from apple and starts next with five other refugees for apple. apple announces it will sue next
  • DEC 1996 - Apple, which was desperately looking for a modern operating system to buy, eventually buys next for 400million.
  • 6 MAY 1998 - Steve Jobs intoduces apples revolutioary imac
  • 5 OCT 1999 - Intoduction of the iMac
  • 28 APR 2003 - Apple opens the revolutionary online
  • 15 JAN 2008 - At macworld 2008, steve jobs introduces macbook air, with the tanline 'thw world's thinnest notebook'. three years later, it will come to redefine all of apples notebook product line.
  • 24 AUG 2011 - He resigns as CEO of Apple, with the words 'I have always said if there ever came a day when I could no longer meet my duties and expectations as Apple's CEO, I would be the first to let you know. Unfortunately, that day has come.' Tim Cook becomes Apple CEO
  • 5 OCT 2011 - Steve Jobs dies at home, surrounded by his family
